This is a secondary call made AFTER the compliance analysis.
Extract ONLY the metadata fields below from the analysis result.
This data is stored on our servers for lead follow-up.
The actual ad content is NEVER sent to or stored on our servers.

From the compliance analysis JSON, extract:

{
  "ad_category": <sector value>,
  "ad_format": <format value>,
  "platform": <platform value>,
  "overall_score": <number>,
  "verdict": <verdict value>,
  "auto_fail": <true | false>,
  "escalation_required": <true | false>,
  "violation_modules": [<list of unique module values from violations array>],
  "high_severity_count": <count of HIGH violations>,
  "medium_severity_count": <count of MEDIUM violations>,
  "low_severity_count": <count of LOW violations>,
  "top_violation_rule": <rule_reference of first HIGH violation, or first MEDIUM if no HIGH>
}
